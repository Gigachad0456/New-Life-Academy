Our Story

Founded with a passion for academic excellence and strong moral values, New Life Ministry School has grown into a trusted institution committed to nurturing confident, responsible, and God-fearing leaders.

We believe education goes beyond the classroom. Our goal is to develop the whole child — academically, spiritually, socially, and physically.
